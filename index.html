Privacy Policy

Currency Converter

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Currency Converter ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

The app may store certain preferences locally on your device:

  • Selected currencies
  • Theme and language settings
  • Transaction fee preferences
  • Historical exchange rate date selections

These preferences remain stored only on your device.

3. Automatically Collected Information

Anonymous technical information may be collected to improve the application:

  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports
  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Application version

This data may be collected using Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics in release versions.

4. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Financial account information
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos

We do not collect personally identifiable information.

5. How Information Is Used

  • Provide accurate currency conversion services
  • Improve app performance
  • Fix crashes and bugs
  • Analyze anonymous usage patterns

6. Data Storage

  • Preferences and cached exchange rates are stored locally on your device.
  • No personal data is stored on remote servers.

Exchange rate data is retrieved from trusted third‑party providers.

7. Third‑Party Services

The application may use the following services:

  • Global Currency Exchange Rates
  • European Central Bank
  • European Commission
  • Central Bank of Norway
  • Central Bank of Russia
  • Bank of Canada
  • Open Exchange Rates
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (Google)

8. Internet Connection

The application requires internet access to retrieve currency exchange rates and update financial data.

9. Children's Privacy

The application does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

10. Security

Reasonable measures are implemented to protect locally stored information. However, no electronic storage method is completely secure.

11. Your Rights

  • You can clear app data through device settings
  • You may uninstall the application at any time
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

12. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Changes will be reflected on this page.
